WHERE I’VE BEEN
I was born and raised in Sydney, Australia and have worked for a handful of computer repairers for the best part of a decade. Turns out I love to fix things! However, I wanted to venture out into the world of academia. The University of Newcastle took me under their wing for a few years studying Biomedical Science in a full-time capacity.


WHAT I’M DOING
I have moved back to Sydney, mid-degree, and have now started a new journey with the University of New South Wales in their Medical Science program part-time. I am lucky to have experienced education through two major universities and look forward to what is ahead. The educators of these institutions provide insight into what my own future may hold.


WHAT I HOPE TO DO
Throughout my life so far there have been people helping and guiding me through my own life. I want to return the favour and help others the way I have been. For me, science is more than a passion, trial and error is a way of life.
